- The high paying Data Anaylst roles in the DFW are mostly financial institutions (Citi, JP Morgan, and Renuity) and big tech companies (Hitachi, Vantara, AT&T, and Cisco)

# Conclusions
The DFW data analytics job market in 2023 shows a healthy mix of opportunity, skill demand, and salary potential. Key takeaways include:

1. **High-Paying Roles:** Leadership and managerial roles ($150kâ€“$185k), while mid-level Senior Analyst roles offer $130kâ€“$150k.
